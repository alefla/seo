Learn html form in 25 Minutes!

Instead of spending your money in coding bootcamp, spend your time to learn coding fast with free videos that we can find on YouTube. I learn html css alot from Web Dev Simplified (WDS) channel. WDS is all about teaching web development skills and techniques in an efficient and practical manner. The teacher is pretty young but I could tell he knows what he is doing. He produced lots of videos on YouTube professionally and he is using the most popular technologies. I found out almost everything that I need to know about web development on his channel. I love the generosity of this guy who shares his knowledge.

The video that I recently watched: learn html form in 25 minutes. He teach us how to create contact form in efficient way. He talked fast and flat to catch up the goal of 25 minutes to cover all of the inputs form. Pause and rewind, that's what I did and it's so worth it watching this video.  We definitely need to learn form with all of those inputs to become a web developer. The basic inputs that most websites use are the inputs for contact form, which allow users to type their name, email address and comment box.  Input type checkbox and radio button also commonly used on form page. One thing that we should not forget is to continue our learning journey to javascript as these inputs mean nothing without javascript.
